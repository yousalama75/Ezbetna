About Ezbetna

SINCE 1986

Your Local Farm

Ezbetna has been dedicated to providing 100% natural buffalo milk products since 1986. Our commitment to quality means we never use hormones or artificial additives in any of our products.

Located in the heart of lush green pastures, our buffaloes graze freely on natural vegetation, resulting in milk that's rich in nutrients and exceptional in taste.

From our farm to your table, we ensure that every product maintains its natural goodness and nutritional value, just as nature intended.

Why Choose Ezbetna

100% Natural

All our products are made from 100% natural buffalo milk with no artificial additives, preservatives, or hormones.

Zero Hormones

We never use hormones in our farming practices, ensuring that our products are as natural and healthy as possible.

Nutritionally Rich

Buffalo milk is naturally higher in protein, calcium, and vitamins compared to cow's milk, making our products more nutritious.

Sustainable Farming

We practice sustainable farming methods that respect the environment and ensure the welfare of our animals.
